Contest Rules

Eligibility

  • Must be a female identifying individual.
  • Must be 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
  • Open internationally (subject to local laws).

Voting Rules

  • Every vote requires a secure payment (Card or Bank Transfer).
  • There is no limit to how many times a single person can vote.
  • Any fraudulent chargebacks will result in deduction of votes for the contestant.

Photo Guidelines

  • No highly edited or digitally altered faces.
  • High quality, well-lit portraits are heavily recommended.
  • No explicit or inappropriate content allowed.
